    Key Characteristics & Facts

    • Domestication: The first animals domesticated by humans, evolving from wolves over 14,000 years ago.
    • Senses: Excellent sense of smell (detecting diseases/explosives), keen hearing, and dichromatic vision (blue/yellow)..
    • Physical Needs: Sweat through paw pads, cool down by panting, and have a third eyelid (nictitating membrane) for eye protection..
    • Breeds: Over 400 breeds exist, from tiny Chihuahuas to giant Great Danes, with mixed breeds called mutts.
